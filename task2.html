Dog Man

Author: Dav Pilkey

₹550

Dog Man" by Dav Pilkey is a hilarious and action-packed graphic novel series aimed primarily at young readers. It follows the adventures of a unique superhero, Dog Man, a part-dog, part-man creature who is the result of a botched police surgery. With the body of a dog and the head of a man, Dog Man is determined to fight crime and protect the world from evil. The series features a blend of slapstick humor, exciting adventures, and heartwarming moments as Dog Man and his loyal companions, like his best friend Petey the Cat (who is both his enemy and occasional ally), battle villains, solve mysteries, and navigate everyday challenges. The books are known for their lively illustrations, which are simple yet expressive, making them accessible to younger readers. With its playful tone, engaging characters, and a strong emphasis on friendship, bravery, and second chances, "Dog Man" is perfect for children, particularly those who enjoy comic books and graphic novels. It’s a lighthearted series that encourages creativity and reading through humor and fun action sequences. The "Dog Man" books are part of a larger collection by Dav Pilkey, who is also known for the "Captain Underpants" series. This makes "Dog Man" a great choice for readers who enjoyed his previous works and are looking for more hilarious, fast-paced stories.

Walking Dead

Author:Robert Kirkman

₹770

The Walking Dead" is a comic book series written by Robert Kirkman, set in a world taken over by zombies, also called "walkers." The story follows Rick Grimes, a sheriff's deputy who wakes up from a coma to find that the world has been destroyed by the undead. As he searches for his family, he meets other survivors, and together they must figure out how to survive in this dangerous new world. The series is about more than just zombies—it explores the struggles of living without laws, the tough choices people have to make, and how humans change when their society falls apart. As the group moves through the ruins of the world, they face both zombie attacks and threats from other groups of survivors who can be just as dangerous. At its heart, The Walking Dead is about survival, trust, and what it means to stay human when everything else is falling apart. The series is known for its intense action, emotional moments, and tough decisions the characters must make.
